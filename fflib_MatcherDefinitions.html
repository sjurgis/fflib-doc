fflib_MatcherDefinitions.SObjectWith

public class SObjectWith implements fflib_IMatcher
SObjectWith matcher: compares the supplied argument against a Map<Schema.SObjectField, Object>, representing fields and their expected values. Note. this method silently catches exceptions getting values for the supplied fields from the arguments supplied in method calls. If your matcher is mysteriously failing for your SObject record, it may be getting silent 'SObject row was retrieved via SOQL without querying the requested field' exceptions, because you haven't queried all of the fields used in this matcher.
